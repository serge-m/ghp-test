Our mission

At VidVac, we want you to start your vacation the moment you board the airplane. We are passionate about finding the hottest spots around the globe and delivering them to you. In order to do this, we are constantly developing the travel-infotainment platform and upgrading our service.

Powered by Airbus

VidVac was launched by the Digital Shapers Unit of Airbus in 2018 and started as a pilot project, combining high quality travel videos produced by some of the world's best content providers with an online community that shares their vacation stories. Soon VidVac became the fastest growing travel-infotainment service in history. Today millions of people around the globe use their personal VidVac profile to enjoy the most fitting experiences and to gather inspiration before arrival.
