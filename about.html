VidVac

At VidVac, we believe there is a better way to spend the time you have in the airplane. A more enjoyable,less invasive way to start your vacation. We are obsessively passionate about it, and our mission is to help people achieve it. Long hard plain rides are one of the ignored aspects of planning a trip, and we see that as an opportunity: We're excited to simplify vacation for everyone through our software, videos, and community.

Our founding

VidVac was founded by AirBus in 2018 and started as an online platform and an online community where some of the world's best content providers share their stories. We launched the VidVac community to provide exclusive destination related content for people to make them able to start their vacation, before arriving there, especially in the plain where you are free to enjoy your lazyness.
